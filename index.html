Privacy Promise
We collect zero data from you. The app runs entirely on your device. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage tracking. What happens in Expense stays in Expense.
Is my data really private?
Yes, absolutely. Expense uses your device's local storage exclusively. No data is ever sent to any server. We don't have servers that could receive your data. Your financial records exist only on your device.
What happens if I forget my PIN?
Since there's no cloud account, PIN recovery works by clearing app data and starting fresh. This is by design — it means no one else can ever access your data either, even if they try to "recover" it.
Can I use the app offline?
Expense works 100% offline. All features — tracking, budgets, reports, analysis — are fully functional without any internet connection. It never needs to sync anything.
How do I clear all my data?
Go to Settings → Data Management → Clear All Data. This instantly removes every transaction, budget and category from your device. The action is permanent. Your PIN is also reset so you can start completely fresh.
How do I change my currency?
Go to Settings → Currency and select from the global currency list. Your choice is saved locally and all amounts will display in your selected currency symbol going forward.
Is the app completely free?
Yes, Expense is completely free to download and use with no limitations. No premium tier, no in-app purchases, no subscription. All 12 features are available from day one at no cost.
Can I change my PIN later?
Yes. Go to Settings → Security → Change PIN. Enter your current PIN first, then set your new one. The change takes effect immediately on your next launch.
