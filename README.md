# Expense - Private Finance Manager

[![Download APK](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-APK-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://expense.codetap.org/Expense.apk)

Expense is a modern, private finance manager designed to help you track income, manage expenses, analyze spending, and stay in control of your personal budget. Built with privacy first, all your data stays on your device, protected by a PIN, and works completely offline.

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Expense Tracking**: Log every purchase with amount, category, date, and notes. Real-time totals and instant categorization.
- **Income Manager**: Record salaries, freelance income, dividends, and more. Track multiple income sources.
- **Debt Tracker**: Manage money you owe and money owed to you. Set due dates and track repayments.
- **Budget Planner**: Set spending limits and monitor progress towards your financial goals.
- **Smart Analytics**: Beautiful charts and spending breakdowns reveal patterns. Weekly, monthly, and yearly views.
- **Reports**: Generate detailed reports on your financial activity.
- **PIN Security**: Protect your data with a personal PIN code.
- **Dark Mode**: Easy on the eyes with a sleek dark theme.
- **Offline Mode**: Works without internet connection.
- **Currency Selection**: Choose from global currencies.
- **Custom Categories**: Create personalized expense categories.
- **Full History**: Access complete transaction history.

## 📥 Installation

1. Visit the official website: [https://expense.codetap.org/](https://expense.codetap.org/)
2. Download the `Expense.apk` file.
3. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your Android settings.
4. Install the APK and launch the app.
5. Set up your PIN and start managing your finances.

**Requirements**: Android device (API 21+ recommended)

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

- **100% Offline**: No internet connection required. All data stored locally on your device.
- **PIN Protected**: Secure access with a personal PIN code.

## ❓ FAQ

### How do I add an expense?

Tap the "+" button, select "Expense", choose category, enter amount and details.

### Can I export my data?

Currently, data export is not available, but all data is stored locally and can be backed up manually.

### Is Expense free?

Yes, completely free with no limitations, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

### How do I change my currency?

Go to Settings → Currency and select from the list.

### How do I clear all data?

Go to Settings → Data Management → Clear All Data. This action is permanent.
